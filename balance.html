Balance

Balance is making sure that a piece of work carries equal weight throughout the aspects of the piece. It is used to settle the viewer, unless you intentionally break rules in order to unsettle the viewer. There are three types of balance in visual media:

  • Radial- Usually refers to circular designs and often ends up in two symmetrical halves.
  • Symmetrical - A bisected work that is a mirror image of the other half.
  • Asymmetrical - When a work is bisected, but the sides are unequal. This leads to a more exciting or interesting piece of work.
