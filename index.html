Overview

This website is designed to help you learn and understand the Six principles of good design (Laid out in Chp. 5 of "White Space is not Your Enemy") and the 4 Laws of Gestalt Theory. The links to all 10 concepts are under the Navigation tab.These concepts are meant to be used in graphic/content design in order to make any piece of visual media eye capturing, informational, and not boring. Take these concepts to heart and practice using them regularly. Doing this will be your first step in becoming a better content designer.

The Six Principles and the 4 Laws consist of these Elements:

6 Principles of Good Design

  • Focal point
  • Contrast
  • Balance
  • Movement
  • Rhythm/Pattern
  • Unity

4 Laws of Gestalt Theory

  • Proximity
  • Similarity
  • Continuity
  • Closure
